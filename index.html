The aim of the game is to feed all 4 trolls hiding under the bridges to the North, South, West and East of the island to distract them so you can escape.

Pick up the food item, approach the bridge and feed the trolls. Once all 4 are fed, 'escape the island'.

Feed all trolls with the correct food to escape.

Hints

  • North troll steals the farmers chickens but only eats the legs
  • East troll is a vegetarian
  • West troll is partial to a sandwich
  • South troll likes burgers in the flavour of the nearby cows
